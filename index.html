    Thomas Crowder

    Thomas Crowder has been licensed as a massage therpist in California since 2014. He has worked for numerous private resorts and individual clients, including the Bernardus Lodge & Spa, Refuge, and Post Ranch Inn. He is trained in Compressive Deep Tissue, Orthopedic techniques for Upper Extremeties, Equine Massage for the horse and rider, Shiatsu, Reflexology, Reiki Energy Healing, and Thai Massage. Thomas utilizes a combination of these therapeutic techniques to curate a unique and uplifting massage experience.
